Who I am

I was born in Barcelona in 1972 and grew up in Subirats (Alt Penedès), in a household shaped by my parents' work as ceramicists and sculptors. At fifteen I returned to Barcelona to study Graphic Arts, sustaining the work through fanzines, independent magazines and an obstinate commitment to photography that has never let up.

I am a photographer, graphic editor and cultural activist based in the Nou Barris district of Barcelona. Over twenty years working in cultural photography: blues, jazz, performing arts, festivals, street. I work by hand — from 35 mm to large-format 10×8" — developing and printing my own work in the darkroom at Nau Bostik.

Photography is not innocent. That is precisely why it matters.

I do not understand photography as decoration but as language: something to be read, edited and, when necessary, challenged. I am interested in images that carry weight, not just surface. Projects that speak of territory, memory and social context.

Keeping analogue photography alive in the digital era is not nostalgia. It is a deliberate stance against speed, excess and the disappearance of photographic craft.

What I do

Commissioned photography

Professional coverage of festivals, concerts, performing arts and cultural events. Official photographer for the Barcelona Blues Festival (2002–2024) and the Barcelona Tattoo Expo (2002–2015). Regular contributor to La Directa and Carrer. Album covers for Barcelona Big Blues Band, Big Dani Pérez, Bernat Font Trio, Joan Pau Cumellas and others.

Graphic editing & photobooks

In 2020 I founded 112 Books, an independent publishing house dedicated to photobooks and photography publications. I work as a graphic editor alongside photographers: cutting, structuring and shaping projects until they become coherent, readable and publishable works. Not every image deserves to be printed. Editing is an act of responsibility.

112books.eu

Photography education

I teach analogue photography, experimental processes and alternative techniques at Llumàtics since 2017 and at the Lomography Embassy Barcelona since 2011. I also organise sessions for Worldwide Pinhole Photography Day at Nau Bostik.

Llumatics.com

Writing & personal projects

I write chronicles at the Pocallum blog: photography, culture, street life. I am finishing L'editor gràfic, an essay on the process of editing images. Jazz and blues enthusiast. Pinhole photographer by deliberate conviction.
